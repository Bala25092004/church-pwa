Our Journey of Faith

Welcome to CSI Ulaga Ratchagar Aalayam in Thiruvarangapatti! Our spiritual journey began humbly in **1950** with a simple thatched roof (கூரை) structure, founded by faithful believers. By God's grace, we constructed our **old church building in 1963**, which served our growing congregation for many years. We joyfully celebrated our **50th Golden Jubilee in 2000**. As our church family continued to expand, the need for a larger space became evident, leading to the construction of our beautiful **new church building in 2016**. The magnificent **Kopuram (கோபுரம்) was added in 2017**, standing tall as a symbol of our faith. We look forward with gratitude to celebrating our **75th Platinum Jubilee in 2025**, marking decades of unwavering service and devotion in this community.we are blessed to build a new church arch and rooftop structure in front of our church, symbolizing our continued faith and growth in Christ.
